# UAE Banking Intelligence Command Centre

A comprehensive analytics dashboard covering the top 10 UAE banks with real 2024 financial data, competitive benchmarking, digital transformation scoring and machine learning forecast to 2027.

**Live Dashboard:** [srikanthdhamodharan.github.io/uae-banking-intelligence](https://srikanthdhamodharan.github.io/uae-banking-intelligence)

---

## Overview

This project independently analyses the UAE banking sector using official annual reports, UAE Central Bank data, KPMG GCC Banking Pulse and Alvarez and Marsal UAE Banking Pulse. It covers all 10 major listed UAE banks and provides executive-level intelligence across five analytical sections.

---

## What the Dashboard Covers

### Section 1: Sector Overview
- Total sector net profit: AED 88 billion in 2024 (up 12.3% vs 2023)
- Total banking assets: AED 4.2 trillion
- Digital banking weekly adoption: 80% of UAE customers
- Green finance issued: USD 11 billion (up 200% since 2022)
- Project financing share: 34% of UAE bank loan books

### Section 2: Bank by Bank Competitive Analysis
- Full scorecard for all 10 UAE listed banks
- Net profit, year-on-year growth rate, digital maturity score
- Strategic positioning and focus area for each bank
- Interactive comparison charts

### Section 3: Digital Transformation Intelligence
- Digital adoption scores for all major UAE banks
- Emirates NBD: 98% of new accounts opened via mobile app
- AI wealth platform growth: 300% user increase in 2024
- Digital wealth transaction volumes: up ninefold at Emirates NBD
- Technology investment breakdown across AI, mobile, cybersecurity and cloud

### Section 4: 2027 Forecast
- ML Gradient Boosting model forecasting sector profit to AED 115 billion by 2027
- Green finance trajectory forecast to USD 28 billion by 2027
- Digital banking adoption forecast reaching 95% by 2027
- Model trained on 2015 to 2024 historical data with R squared of 0.94

### Section 5: Executive Intelligence Brief
- Six strategic insights covering market concentration, digital transformation correlation, green finance opportunity, regional expansion, AI adoption and data analytics talent demand
- Strategic health radar chart comparing Emirates NBD, FAB and Mashreq

---

## Key Findings

- Emirates NBD reported record profit before tax of AED 27.1 billion in 2024
- FAB and Emirates NBD together hold 45% of total UAE banking assets
- Mashreq achieved the highest growth rate at 27.8% year-on-year in 2024
- Banks with digital scores above 90 averaged 22.6% profit growth vs 14.1% for banks below 80
- Data and analytics roles in UAE grew 1,006% from 2015 to 2024
